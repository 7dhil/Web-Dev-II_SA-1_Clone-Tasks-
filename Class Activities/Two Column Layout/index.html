Toyota Supra MK4

Powered by the legendary Toyota 2JZ engine, the MK4 combined power, a nearly limitless power ceiling, and Toyota reliability. It’s one of a handful of JDM performance cars that is responsible for inspiring generations of racers who were born well after the car’s initial debut.

Nissan GTR

The Nissan Skyline GT-R is a Japanese sports car based on the Nissan Skyline range. The first cars named "Skyline GT-R" were produced between 1969 and 1972 under the model code KPGC10, and were successful in Japanese touring car racing events.

Civic EG6

The fifth-generation Honda Civic is an automobile produced by Honda from 1991 until 1995. It debuted in Japan on September 9, 1991. At its introduction, it won the Car of the Year Japan award for the second time.
